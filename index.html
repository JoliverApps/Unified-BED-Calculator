Unified BED Calculator

Compute equivalent dose for a new fractionation scheme using the RD framework:

$$ \mathrm{BED}(D,n;r,s) = \frac{D}{1-r} - \frac{r}{s(1-r)}\,n\Bigl(1-e^{-s D/n}\Bigr) $$
